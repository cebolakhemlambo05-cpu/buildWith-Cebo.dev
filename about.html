Who I am

Cebolakhe Mlambo

I'm a software developer based in South Africa, with a broad technical background that spans from system-level programming all the way to consumer-facing web and mobile applications.

My foundation in system software and communication networks gives me something most web developers don't have: a genuine understanding of what's happening underneath the abstraction. When I build an app, I know how data moves, where bottlenecks live and why infrastructure decisions matter.

On top of that, I've built expertise in full-stack web development — HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, SQL, MongoDB and Firebase — as well as mobile development in C# and Python, and information systems design for business contexts.

I don't just make software that works. I make software that's worth maintaining.

I'm currently open to employment, freelance projects and technical collaborations. If you have something worth building, let's talk.
